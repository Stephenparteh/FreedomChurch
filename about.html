Beliefs

Our church holds to the following core beliefs:

  • The Trinity: We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • The Bible: We believe that the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.
  • Salvation: We believe that salvation is a gift from God, received through faith in Jesus Christ alone.
  • The Church: We believe in the universal Church, the body of Christ, and the local church as an expression of the universal Church.
  • Baptism and Communion: We practice baptism by immersion and regularly observe the Lord's Supper.

Mission & Vision

  • Mission: Our mission is to love God, love people, and make disciples of Jesus Christ through worship, teaching, and service.
  • Vision: We envision a church that transforms lives and communities through the power of the Gospel, fosters deep relationships, and equips every believer to live out their God-given purpose.
