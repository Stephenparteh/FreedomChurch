Welcome to National Freedom Pentecostal Church

Join us in our mission to spread love, faith, and hope to our community and beyond.

Our Vision & Mission

At National Freedom Pentecostal Church, our vision is to spread the word of God and serve our community with compassion and integrity. Our mission is to inspire and empower individuals to live a life that reflects Christ’s love and teachings. We believe in fostering a supportive and inclusive environment where everyone can grow spiritually and make a positive impact in their communities.

History

  • Founding Year

    National Freedom Pentecostal Church was founded in 1990 with a commitment to serving the local community through worship and outreach programs. The initial congregation was small, but the mission to spread the gospel grew quickly.

  • Major Expansion

    In 2005, the church expanded its facilities to accommodate growing numbers and added new ministries to better serve the community. This expansion included a new auditorium and additional classrooms for Bible studies and community events.

  • Community Outreach

    Over the years, we have launched numerous community outreach programs, including food drives, youth mentorship initiatives, and support for local schools. Our commitment to community service is a cornerstone of our church’s mission.

  • Global Missions

    In recent years, we have expanded our mission efforts globally, partnering with international organizations to support humanitarian projects and spread the message of Christ worldwide.

Our Faith

We believe in the fundamental teachings of Christianity and uphold the values of faith, hope, and love. Our church is committed to living out the teachings of Jesus Christ and promoting a life of service and compassion. Here are some key aspects of our faith:

Scripture

We hold the Bible as the inspired word of God and the foundation of our beliefs and practices. It guides our faith and teaches us how to live a Christ-centered life.

Love

We believe in loving our neighbors as ourselves, showing compassion, and serving others with kindness. Love is at the heart of our mission and interactions.

Prayer

Prayer is a vital part of our spiritual life, connecting us with God and seeking His guidance. We encourage prayer as a means to strengthen our faith and resolve.

Community

We value building a supportive and inclusive community where everyone can grow spiritually and make a positive impact. Our church is a place of fellowship and mutual support.
